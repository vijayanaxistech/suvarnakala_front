Privacy Policy

Suvarnakala Pvt. Ltd. respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is collected and used. By accessing the services provided by suvarnakala.com, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at sales@suvarnakala.com.

This policy does not apply to entities or individuals not managed, owned, or controlled by Suvarnakala Pvt. Ltd.

Our Commitment

We aim to:

  • Ensure you feel at ease while using our websites.
  • Build confidence in sharing information with us.
  • Make you aware that you can contact us with privacy concerns.
  • Inform you that using our websites implies agreement to data collection as outlined.

Information We Collect

We collect details provided during registration, information from your use of our services, and visits to our website or linked sites. We do not access or store your Payment Card details (e.g., credit/debit card number, expiration date, CVV). These are securely transmitted to your card issuer for authorization.

Additional information may be collected from promotions, contests, feedback, or profile forms. We monitor traffic patterns and website usage to improve our services. Only necessary and relevant information is collected.

Automatically collected anonymous information includes:

  • Computer-identification information
  • IP address
  • Domain server
  • Type of computer and web browser

Personally identifiable information may include:

  • First and last name
  • Email addresses
  • Contact details (phone numbers, PIN/ZIP code)
  • Demographic profile (age, gender, occupation, education, address)
  • Personal preferences and interests
  • Opinions on our services, products, and features

We also track pages visited, links clicked, items added to bag or wishlist, and other websites opened simultaneously.

How We Collect Information

Anonymous traffic information is collected when you visit our website. Personally identifiable information is collected during registration, guest checkout, online surveys, or contests. Required information includes name, email, date of birth, gender, billing/delivery address, PIN code, and mobile number.

We use this information to contact you about services, fulfill orders, and process payments. We are the sole owners of the information collected.

How We Use Your Information

Contact information is used to send:

  • Password reminders and registration confirmations
  • Special offers
  • Changes in service policies or terms of use
  • Order information, renewal notices, invites, and reminders

Personal information is used to:

  • Enhance personalized features
  • Contact you for account-related matters
  • Deliver requested services (e.g., merchandise)
  • Preserve social history as required by law

Anonymous traffic information helps us deliver personalized services, manage website functionality, and understand user behavior.

Who We Share Information With

We do not share or rent your information with third parties except our agents and alliance partners, such as shipping companies and payment processors, who use it only to fulfill orders. Alliance partners may use email addresses to confirm deliveries or offer related services.

We may disclose information if required by law, to investigate illegal activities, or with your permission to provide requested products or services.

Cookies

We use session and persistent cookies to enhance security and user experience. Session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies store preferences like username and interests. You can reject cookies, but this may limit website functionality. Cookies do not contain personal information unless you provide it.

Security

We follow industry standards to protect your information during transmission and storage, but no method is 100% secure. Our servers are password-protected and accessible only to authorized personnel. Information is shared on a need-to-know basis to complete transactions.

Managing Your Information

You can update or correct your information online or by emailing sales@suvarnakala.com. To terminate your account, email us with “Please close my suvarnakala.com account” in the subject line. Your account will be disabled within 2 working days, though information may remain in our archives.

You can opt-out of marketing communications via “My Account” or by contacting us. Service-related communications are mandatory and cannot be opted out.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy, with changes posted on our website. Material changes will be notified on the homepage or other appropriate places. Information use will remain consistent with the policy under which it was collected.

Contact Us

For questions or feedback, contact Suvarnakala Pvt. Ltd. at sales@suvarnakala.com.
